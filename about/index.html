About TaskAgents

A specialized consultancy building production AI systems

Who We Are

TaskAgents is a small, senior engineering team focused on a specific problem: helping organizations in regulated industries actually ship AI systems to production.

We're not a large firm with juniors doing the work. We're not generalists who dabble in AI alongside other services. We're specialists who've been building LLM applications, RAG systems, and intelligent automation since before it was called "AI agents."

Our team brings deep experience from high-stakes environments where "move fast and break things" isn't an option. We understand compliance requirements, audit trails, data governance, and the operational discipline required to run production AI systems in regulated industries.

What Makes Us Different

Senior Practitioners, Not a Sales Org

The people who scope your project are the people who build it. No hand-offs to offshore teams or junior developers. We staff engagements with engineers who have built these systems before.

Specialization in Regulated Industries

We understand compliance frameworks, security reviews, and audit requirements. We know what questions compliance teams ask and how to build systems that pass those reviews.

Production-First Mindset

Demos are easy. Production is hard. We design for observability, error handling, cost management, and maintainability from day one, not as an afterthought when the pilot needs to scale.

Vendor-Neutral Guidance

We're not reselling a specific platform or locked into a single vendor. We help you choose the right tools for your requirements, whether that's OpenAI, Anthropic, open source models, or a hybrid approach.

Transparent About Limitations

AI can't solve every problem. We'll tell you if your use case is a poor fit, if you need better data first, or if there's a simpler non-AI solution. Our goal is to deliver value, not sell you projects.

Knowledge Transfer Built In

We don't create dependency. Every engagement includes documentation, training, and operational handoff so your team can own and evolve the system.

Our Approach

Start with the Problem, Not the Solution

We don't start by asking "What can we do with AI?" We start with your specific business problem, understand the constraints and requirements, then determine if AI is the right approach and what architecture makes sense.

Build for Production from Day One

Pilots that can't scale to production are expensive science projects. We design systems with production requirements in mind from the beginning. Error handling, monitoring, cost management, and security are considered even in the initial pilot phase.

Iterate Based on Real Usage

We ship working software quickly and refine based on actual user feedback. Weekly demos, rapid iteration, and continuous measurement keep projects on track and aligned with real needs rather than assumptions.

Transfer Knowledge, Not Dependency

Our goal is to make you successful, not dependent on us. Every project includes comprehensive documentation, operational runbooks, and hands-on training so your team can own and evolve the system after we're gone.
