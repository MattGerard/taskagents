Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with startups or only enterprise?

We work with organizations of all sizes, as long as the project is a good fit for our expertise. The key factors are: clear business problem, available data/access, and realistic expectations about AI capabilities.

What's your typical engagement size?

Our projects range from 1-2 week assessments to multi-month implementations. We're flexible on engagement models: fixed-scope projects, retainers, or augmented team arrangements.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign mutual NDAs before discussing confidential information.

What if we're not sure AI is the right solution?

That's exactly why we offer Opportunity Assessments. We'll help you figure out if AI makes sense, what alternatives exist, and what the realistic ROI looks like.
